## Boolean Basics 
The boolean algebra is used to represnt and simplify digital circuit it works with  only two values.

### The basics terms 
- variable A , B , c
- complement A' (NOT A)
- And A * B
- Or A + B

## Basic operations 

- And operation
       A*B = 1 when atleast both are '1'

- OR operation
       A+B = 1 when atleast one is '1'

- NOT operation
       A. opposite of A
