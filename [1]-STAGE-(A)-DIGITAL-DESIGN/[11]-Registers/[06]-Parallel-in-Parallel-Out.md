## PIPO 
A PIPO (Parallel-in-Parallel-Out) shift register are digital circuits that load and output all data bits simulteniously within a single clock cycle, actiin as fast buffer storage.

### Example: 4-Bit PIPO Shift Register

- Structure: It consists of four D flip-flop (D0 to D3) connected in parallel.

- Input/Output: Data bits (e.g,1011) are applied to inputs.

- Operation: Unlike PISO or SIPO, data is not shifted left or right; it is immediately available.
