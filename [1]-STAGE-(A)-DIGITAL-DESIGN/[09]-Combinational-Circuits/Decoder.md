## Decoder
A decoder is a combinational logic circuit that converts n inputs lines a maximum of 2n unique output lines acting as a translator that activates one specfic output based on the binary input code.

#### Types Of Secoder
- 2 - to 4
- 3 - to - 8 
- 4 - to - 16
