## Multiplexer 

A multiplexer (MUX) is a combinational logic circuit that acts as a high speed digital switch, selecting one of many input signals and routing it to a single output line. 

### MUX Work On
A MUX has 2n inputs, n selection lines, and 1 output. for example, a 4 - to - 1 uses 2 select lines to choose between 4 inputs lines .

### Types
- 2 - to - 1
- 4 - to - 1
- 8 - to - 1
- 16 - to - 1
